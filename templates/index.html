Bigger Brother is watching you!



Do you feel like you are being watched?

This website is made to help you find out. Closed-circuit television (CCTV) systems are increasingly widespread. We might not be aware of this at first, but as soon as you start looking around, you cannot un-see it anymore. Both inside and outside you are surrounded by signs or cameras watching you. Big Brother has become a reality.

We decided to increase awareness of this fact through creating a platform where we can collectively track the CCTV surveillance down. Thus, watching Big Brother itself and becoming a Bigger Brother. By uploading pictures of CCTV signs together with their locations we can create a database containing all CCTV signs.

The artist Daniel Buzzo made an incredible start by photographing CCTV signs and noting down their locations in several countries all by himself. You can view his map and gallery on signsofsurveillance.com/.
