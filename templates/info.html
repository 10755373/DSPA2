Bigger Brother is watching you!



Do you feel like you are being watched?

This website is made to help you find out. Closed-circuit television (CCTV) systems are increasingly widespread. We might not be aware of this at first, but as soon as you start looking around, you cannot un-see it anymore. Both inside and outside you are surrounded by signs or cameras watching you. Big Brother has become a reality.

We decided to increase awareness of this fact through creating a platform where we can collectively track the CCTV surveillance down. Thus, we will be Bigger Brother.

The artist Daniel Buzzo made an incredible start by photographing CCTV signs and noting down their locations in several countries all by himself. You can view his map and gallery on signsofsurveillance.com.

Why the awareness of CCTV surveillance must grow.



With the increase of technology use across the globe, closed-circuit television (CCTV) surveillance is also becoming increasingly widespread. This trend should, however, not be taken lightly.

There are certain claimed benefits to CCTV. For example, it is said to reduce crime, prevent terrorism and increase marketing profits. But many of the beneficial claims have been partly or entirely disproven or come with significant side effects. The drawbacks and dangers of widespread CCTV are, on the other side, backed up by scientific studies. Most of the hazards may not lie in the use of CCTV itself but in how the humans behind the cameras deal with it. Discrimination, invasion of privacy, totalitarian monitoring, data leaks, fraud, and perceived impaired freedom of expression are all worrisome side effects of the increase of CCTV surveillance.

To cope with CCTV ethically and safely, people must gain more awareness about the current state of affairs and possible future scenarios regarding CCTV use.
